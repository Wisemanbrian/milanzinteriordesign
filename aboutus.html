our STORY

Founded In 2020 By Samantha Heaven Milanzi, Milanzi Interiors Has Become One Of Malawi’s Most Reputable Design Studios. Known For Its Eclectic, Sustainable, And Soulful Approach, The Firm Delivers One-Of-A-Kind Spaces Defined By Detail, Craftsmanship, And Authenticity.

With Over 8 Years Of Industry Experience, Our Team Brings A Unique Blend Of International Design Trends And Deep Appreciation For Malawian Culture And Craftsmanship.

Every Project Is An Opportunity To Create Something Extraordinary—Spaces That Don’t Just Look Beautiful, But Feel Right, Function Seamlessly, And Tell A Meaningful Story.
